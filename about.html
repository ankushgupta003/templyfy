About Templyfy

Making Work Smarter, Not Harder

At Templyfy, we believe productivity shouldn’t be complicated. Our mission is to help students, entrepreneurs, and professionals save time, stay organized, and work smarter using ready-to-use Google Sheets and Microsoft Excel templates.

Whether you're managing a project, tracking expenses, or planning operations — our templates simplify everything. With Templyfy, you don’t just manage data; you master it.

Our Story

Like many of you, I struggled with endless spreadsheets, scattered project data, and wasted hours fixing formulas. Out of that frustration came curiosity — and soon, a passion for automation.

I began experimenting with Excel macros and Google Sheets automations to streamline my workflow. Each template I created solved a real problem — from task tracking to time management — and that’s how Templyfy was born.

What started as a personal productivity project is now a growing collection of smart, automated templates trusted by students, startups, and professionals worldwide.

Why Choose Templyfy

  • Ready-to-use templates — No setup or coding required.
  • Smartly designed for real-world use cases.
  • Time-saving automation with built-in formulas & macros.
  • Affordable & reliable — Premium quality at student-friendly prices.
  • Instant access & lifetime updates — No subscriptions or hidden fees.

Our Mission

We aim to make productivity tools simple, affordable, and accessible to everyone — from students managing coursework to entrepreneurs running businesses. Templyfy helps you focus on your goals, not the repetitive work behind them.
